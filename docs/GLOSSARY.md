# JOLT Service Glossary (v1.00 Premium OS)

## System Objects

| Term | Everyday Explanation |
|------|----------------------|
| **Tenant** | A landlord or building owner group. It keeps their data separate from everyone else. |
| **Site** | A specific building or location where a machine is kept. |
| **Shipment** | A delivery of supplies sent to one specific building. |
| **Box** | One of the boxes in a shipment. |
| **Proof** | Clear evidence that a job was done right (Photos, GPS, Video, Scans). |
| **Evidence Packet** | A folder that bundles all the proof for a delivery to settle any arguments. |
| **Incident** | A problem that the system noticed and needs someone to fix. |

## v1.00 Premium OS Terms

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **PARTNER_TECHNICIAN** | Field role responsible for site maintenance, deliveries, and refills. |
| **Soft Warnings** | Dismissible alerts for non-compliance. Technicians can proceed, but the event is logged for Ops Manager review. |
| **Workflow Airlock** | Feature that hard blocks app usage if unsynced data is older than 1 hour. This is the only hard lock in the system. |
| **Points** | Engagement currency won via Lucky Spins. 500 points = 1 Bonus Spin. |
| **Vacation Race** | Annual competition where the top cumulative score wins an ALL EXPENSES PAID BEACH GETAWAY. |
| **Lucky Spin** | Animated reward wheel for fast delivery acceptance. |

## Core Concepts

| Term | Everyday Explanation |
|------|----------------------|
| **Watchdog** | An automatic check that runs every 60 minutes looking for rule violations. It logs Soft Warnings. |
| **Action Rail** | The bar at the top of the app showing your most important next task and potential rewards. |
| **ProofAssist** | The smart camera that checks photo quality before letting you capture. |
| **Geofence** | The 50-meter invisible boundary around a site. POD photos must be taken inside it. |
| **Sync SLA** | The 1-hour deadline for your phone to upload data to the server. |
| **Ghost Delivery** | Unattended drop-off using site-specific access codes with GPS and photo proof. |

## Hardware & Maintenance

| Term | Everyday Explanation |
|------|----------------------|
| **Jolt Automated Barista** | The coffee machine you are maintaining. |
| **Dirty Water** | The water that needs to be emptied from the machine. |
| **Trash** | The used grounds or waste inside the machine. |
| **Inside Floor** | The bottom interior surface of the machine. |

## Roles (v1.00 Finalized)

| Role | Who They Are |
|------|--------------|
| **PARTNER_TECHNICIAN** | The person on-site who accepts deliveries and refills the machine. |
| **DRIVER** | The person who drops off boxes and scans them at the building. |
| **OPS_MANAGER** | The person in the main office who packs boxes, manages users, and monitors performance. |
| **LANDLORD_VIEWER** | The building owner who can see reports but can't change anything. |

## Status Colors

| Color | What It Means |
|-------|---------------|
| **Green** | Everything is good. Site is ACTIVE, all tasks are current. |
| **Yellow** | Needs attention. Pending tasks or soft warnings present. |
| **Red** | Urgent issue. Overdue tasks or sync failure. |
| **Blue** | Action in progress or required. |
