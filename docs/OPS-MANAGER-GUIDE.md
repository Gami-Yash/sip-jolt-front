# OPS_MANAGER Console Guide (v1.01 Gate5 OS)

## Your Role

You oversee the entire SIPJOLT operation. In v1.00, you focus on monitoring performance and supporting technicians through incentives rather than strict lockouts.

---

## Part 1: v1.00 Soft Warnings

Lockouts (SAFE_MODE/UILockOverlay) have been removed in favor of **Soft Warnings**.
- **Visibility**: View the "Ops Warnings" dashboard to see all warnings dismissed by technicians.
- **Trend Analysis**: Monitor which technicians frequently ignore warnings.
- **Enforcement**: Compliance issues automatically reduce the technician's **Vacation Score** and disqualify them from **Monthly Perfection** bonuses.

---

## Part 2: The v1.00 Incentive System

### Lucky Spins & Gamification
Monitor technician engagement through the Gamification Dashboard.
- **TIER 1 (2h)**: +2 Spins
- **TIER 2 (6h)**: +1 Spin
- **Engagement**: 500 Points → +1 Bonus Spin

### Monthly Perfection
Track which technicians are on track for the +3 Bonus Spin "Perfection" award. 
Criteria: 20+ deliveries, 80% speed rate, zero quality issues, all refills on time.

---

## Part 3: Watchdog v1.00 (Monitor & Alert)

The Watchdog runs every 60 minutes.
- **Compliance Tracking**: Logs all ignored alerts and overdue tasks.
- **Sync SLA (>1h)**: Still triggers the **Workflow Airlock** on the technician's device. This remains a hard requirement for data integrity.

---

## Part 4: 5-Gate QC Packing

Ensure all shipments pass the 5-Gate QC wizard:
1. Zero Check (Calibration)
2. Freshness Lock (Oxygen Absorbers)
3. Weight Law (46.5 lb Hard Limit)
4. Movement Test (Shake/Compression)
5. Envelope Sticker (Color Code)
