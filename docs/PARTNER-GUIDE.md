# PARTNER_TECHNICIAN Field Guide (v1.01 Gate5 OS)

## Your Role

You keep the Jolt Automated Barista machines running smoothly. Your main jobs are:
- Accept or refuse deliveries (Earn Lucky Spins!)
- Refill the supply closet and document it with photos
- Keep your **Coffee Perk** unlocked by staying current on tasks
- Compete in the **Vacation Race** (ALL EXPENSES PAID BEACH GETAWAY)
- Complete weekly check-ins and monthly cleans

---

## Part 1: The Action Rail & Pull-Based Incentives

The app is designed to reward you for high performance. No more lockdowns—just rewards.

### Speed Bonuses (Lucky Spins)
The faster you accept a delivery, the more spins you get:
- **TIER 1 (Under 2 hours)**: +2 Lucky Spins 🏆
- **TIER 2 (Under 6 hours)**: +1 Lucky Spin ⚡

### The Coffee Perk ☕
Stay on top of your tasks to unlock your daily free coffee. 
- **Unlocked**: No overdue tasks (delivery acceptance or visits < 24h).
- **Locked**: Pending overdue tasks or urgent alerts.

---

## Part 2: Accepting Deliveries

When supplies arrive, check the Action Rail. It will tell you which bonus tier is active.

### The Delivery Acceptance Workflow
1. **Review Delivery**: See what was delivered.
2. **Inspect Boxes**: Count and check.
3. **Accept**: Tap to accept and claim your bonus spins!
4. **Placement Photo**: Take a photo of where you put the boxes.

**No App Lockouts**: Pending tasks and warnings are dismissible. You can always keep working, but non-compliance is logged for the Ops Manager and affects your Vacation Score.

---

## Part 3: Workflow Airlock (Sync SLA)

The only time the app will lock is for **Sync Issues**. 
- Your data must sync within **1 hour** of completing a task.
- If it doesn't, the **Workflow Airlock** blocks all navigation to protect data integrity.
- Tap "Retry Sync" when you have a better signal to unlock the app.

---

## Part 4: Vacation Race & Points

### Points to Spins
- Win points on the Lucky Spin wheel (+25, +50, +100).
- Every **500 points** automatically converts to **+1 Bonus Spin**.

### The Vacation Race
- Your performance is tracked all year.
- The technician with the highest score on Dec 31st wins an **ALL EXPENSES PAID BEACH GETAWAY**.
- Score is based on: On-time rate, Quality, Volume, and Consistency.
